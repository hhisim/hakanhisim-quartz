Workflow timelapse and creation process of 10 hours compressed into 8 Minutes.

I started this sub-series in the Universal Transmissions style about seven years ago with the artwork titled; "[[Bio-Energetic Vortexes No.1 - Root (Root Chakra)]]" 

It depicts and visualises my personal experiences of the Root Muladhara Chakra. This fascination started a 7-year adventure of creating an image for each Vortex of the 7 Chakra energy centre system. 

Yesterday, that voyage ended, and the cycle was completed as I finished creating; "Bio-Energetic Vortexes No:7 - Spirit" is a personal rendition of the Sahasrara or "Crown" Chakra. 

Though every one of these seven images was challenging to create, this final seventh "seal" was particularly challenging. 

This was because I experienced this gateway's full-blown epic power last year in 2021. During a series of awakenings into states of Samadhi, and in one the darkest places imaginable, during the most trying time of my life. The profoundly transformative comes to mind. However, there are and can never be any words for that luminously simple state of being—childlike innocence and wonder paired with Godlike awareness and wisdom. 

The artwork "[[Bio-Energetic Vortexes No.7 - Spirit (Crown Chakra)]]" is not an interpretation of Samadhi. It is a visual journal entry into my intimate experience of the "Thousand Petal Lotus" opening. It is a homage to my experience of basking in the glory of divine consciousness. 

The Gnostic current flowed strong. I had many realisations and personal awakenings, but what stuck with me was that Joy is the seed or centre of suffering. Joy is such a preciously innocent and beautiful thing it had to be encased and "protected" by many onion-like layers of suffering, pain and misery. When one willfully surrenders into the heart of that pain and suffering, that seed of joy would then bloom and transform both the pain and the person. 

Joy and misery are different sides of the same coin. I am clarifying the presence of "Kundalini Serpents "Ida" and "Pingala" in this artwork. 

Something else I realised was that Muladhara is not the actual seat of Kundalini. Although many classic descriptions portray "Muladhara Chakra" as the root that awakens Kundalini  [[Kundalini Rising]] ) as it rises up the spinal column. 

In my experience and later more profound research, I found that the "seat of Kundalini is Sahasrara". Muladhara is only a manipulation centre or a switch like the other energy centres. Still, it is usually the easiest to turn on. Each chakra is independent, and whichever chakra awakens, the kundalini shakti directly travels towards the root of the mystical tree, the Sahasrara chakra.
