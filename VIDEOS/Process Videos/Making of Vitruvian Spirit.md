 ### **Making of Vitruvian Spirit**

Workflow time lapse and creation process of **27 hours** compressed into **14 Minutes** (**2 months**) 

I want to make a clear statement regarding this artwork as I feel It could be misunderstood and misconstrued in these tumultuous times when the meanings of many concepts in our global culture have been subverted, hijacked and at times inverted. 

This piece is not about gender, it is not about the superiority of any gender or role, It contains a message of balance, of the power of the divine feminine and masculine and of the balanced trinity of male female and the androgen within us all. 

This is a homage to the masters of antiquity who have inspired generations to create and innovate, it is a celebration of the human spirit and imagination. We have the capacity to be the fool, the jester, the joker. We have the will and power to either be a slave or a sovereign being, we can either be puppets controlled by the invisible threads of "would be controllers" or we can rise up to claim our birthrights as sovereign monarchs of our own individual consciousness. 

The choice is ours, and always has been. The year 2020 was a clear wake-up call for humanity to rise up and take responsibility for ourselves and our place in the cosmos. I firmly believe have risen to the challenge and are forging our new path as navigators of the cosmic ocean. 

We are the exemplar, the immaculate conception, the apex being, we are the Mercurial Bodhisattva, we are the Kwisatz Haderach. I know this is a very bold statement, but it rings true, and I wanted to create something to inspire to seed the new Renaissance into a new golden age of human ingenuity, of sustainable innovation and of true balance. 

I was going to acknowledge the genius contemporaries who are alive right now and are actively working to reshape our world into something we truly deserve. There are too many names to mention, and making a list of influences could lead to favoritism and in all honesty I would probably have forgotten to mention many who have made a mark on our culture. 

You all know who you are, and I have the utmost respect and admiration for all of you. Wholeness, balance and infinite gratitude to all of you. **Hakan HISIM.**
