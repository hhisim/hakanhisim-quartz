### **Messiah**

A Dark take on religion, the occult and dominator culture... 

Messiah, is an experimental short, questioning the existence of Orthodox Religion, the Suppression of the Female and the Justification of the Sacred Feminine. 

screened at experimental film festivals around the world. The first Experimental stop-motion animation from Hakan Hisim.

Made during my early to mid 20's - Full of angst, ready to revolt and dark as fuck. The seed of my current art exists in these early pieces.
