### **The Insiders**

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/R4xQXmzcxk8?si=1XWBwLnaHPzh3MR2" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" referrerpolicy="strict-origin-when-cross-origin" allowfullscreen></iframe>

The insiders is an experimental film dealing with media and television addiction. 

It shows a man watching himself reading a book on the TV screen and his endless attempts to escape this never ending nightmare and vicious circle. 

Was Screened between 1-7th April 2004 at the International Istanbul Short Film Festival., and between 12-19 March 2005 at the 2nd Yildiz Short Film Festival. Receiving 2 awards.

Made during my early to mid 20's - Full of angst, ready to revolt and dark as fuck. The seed of my current art exists in these early pieces.
