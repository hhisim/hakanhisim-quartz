### **The Kayisharga Documentaries**

Already Accepted as Cult status, The Kayisharga Documentaries is an experimental film posing as a documentary dealing with a nonexistent species called 'The Kayisharga'. It is an absurd stand against the documentary format, Organized Religion, Conventional cinema, and the system. 

Screened at many strange places...

Made during my early to mid 20's - Full of angst, ready to revolt and dark as fuck. The seed of my current art exists in these early pieces.
