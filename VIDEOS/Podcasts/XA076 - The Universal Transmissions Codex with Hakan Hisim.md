On this episode Matt is joined by Hakan Hisim. Hakan is a visionary artist who is inspired by trans-dimensional linguistics, esoteric literature, the occult, out-of-body experiences, dimensional travel and the akashic records. Hakan recently completed his magnum opus, The [[The Universal Transmissions Codex Vol.1 - The Unwritten Book that cannot be read.]], which is a collection of his many visionary works exploring xenolinguistics (extraterrestrial language) and the glyphs, shapes and symbols that psychonauts often report seeing during experiences with entheogens.

During the conversation the two discuss how Hakan began as an artist and the mind-blowing experiences that inspired him to begin creating. They then dive into their respective experiences with the spirit molecule, DMT, and whether or not the compound can be produced endogenously. The two then discuss Hakan's experience of Samadhi, Kundalini Awakening, MXE (an extremely rare psychedelic dissociative) and of course The Universal Transmissions Codex, which is available now in limited quantities. This episode dives deep into the rabbit hole. Please enjoy!
