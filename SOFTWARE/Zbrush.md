ZBrush has been an absolute game-changer for me. In fact, it's probably still the most enjoyable piece of software I've ever used.

It revolutionized how I approached my compositions, transforming my workflow and elevating the color, texture, and depth of my 3D models and compositions.

ZBrush was also the primary reason I started using a Wacom tablet. It became to 3D what [[Corel Painter ]]was to 2D—a fundamental shift in how I approached my art.

Although I primarily use it to enhance almost everything I do in 3D, I rarely use it as a standalone tool. However, I'm intrigued by the idea of exploring its standalone capabilities further.
