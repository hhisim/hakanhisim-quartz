## Narrative:
The music of Entheogenic produces emotions that bridge different levels of awareness causing feelings of inspiration on a spiritual manner. They formed at 1999 and released 4 full length and one remixed album defining in large part the sound of Psychill. Soon after their last album (Flight Of The Urubus) they split, with Helmut relocating to Brazil.  
​  
After a 3 year break Piers moved back to the studio, this time alone, releasing [[Entheogenic - Gaia Sophia]]. This was the first album of Entheogenic available only digitally. Unfortunately that pattern continued for all the new releases. Fast forward to 2015 there is a total of 3 albums and one EP available only digitally.  
​  
Digital Reprints is here to quench the thirst of collectors. All 3 albums ([[Entheogenic - Gaia Sophia]][2011], [[Entheogenic - Enthymesis]] [2014], [[Entheogenic - A Singularity Encoded]][2015]), 2 EPs (B-Sides [2008], [[Entheogenic - Anthropomorphic]] [2013]) and a bonus rmx by Kuba will be released on physical format.  
​  
8-panel high quality digipack, an 8pp booklet and a slipcase, all with artwork of Hakan Hisim will compliment the sound of Entheogenic in a truly artistic way. Only 100 copies will be printed and made available exclusively to Kickstarter backers. Those backers will be credited in the booklet.
