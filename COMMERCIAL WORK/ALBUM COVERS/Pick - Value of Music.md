### Pick - Value of Music
![[0933871D-5BE2-2446-8C1A-B84CDCD56850.jpg]]
## Narrative:
Pick is releasing his 8th album “Value Of Music” on Jaira Records. The Israeli deep trance producer Pick is back, releasing this album after three years of intensive work. And he is on a mission.  
  
“Music is a very important ritual. My main mission as an artists is to make people dive into this analog deep and in the same time light trip”  
  
The album ''Value Of Music'' consisting of one intro and seven more tracks, was made between 2012 and 2015. Pick is back with endless energy and enthusiasm to redefine the light side of Dark Progressive,  
Massive sound, clean compositions, grooves surrounding by sparkling dynamics and following with classic & analog elements, will launch you straight into the psychedelic deep realms.
