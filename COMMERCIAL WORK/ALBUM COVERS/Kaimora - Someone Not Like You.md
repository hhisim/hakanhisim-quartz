## Narrative:
**The Band:**
As a Band we are Open Source: talented artists of all kinds are welcome to contribute in whatever way inspires them – a song, an album, a live performance, or more. Working together in an open, creative environment we joyfully tap into the Collective Muse.

**The Concept:**
The Kaimora Concept is the essence of the ancient Monomyth and the exploration of the Sacred Worlds. A place of infinite possibilities limited only by imagination. We aim to inspire spiritual awakening through creative expression.

**The Realm**
The Kaimora Realm is an ever-evolving unfoldment in our lives. It is a sacred place that will ultimately span the Physical World, The Web, the Metaverse; a fluid dimension where together we create, share, perform, and inspire.
