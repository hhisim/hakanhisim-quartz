### **Nanosphere - Twisted Clarity**
![[nanosphere.jpg]]
## Narrative:
Deep, squelchy and super-groovy psychedelic mid-tempo is the order of the day, and Nanosphere is serving up 4 tasty courses.  
  
Known for his twisted, uncompromising psybreaks on Broken Robot records, Sam has transposed his distinctive high-energy sound down a notch into 110bpm territory for this EP, making full use of the extra space, bass and groove to get you moving in a whole new way.  
  
The result is four slick progressive tracks that explore the overlap between psytrance and deep mid-tempo breaks.
