## Narrative:

It has been a little bit over a year now. What started as a little dream became a beautiful reality. A 4xCD box set of the beautiful sounds of Entheogenic. With your help, followed box sets for Kaya Project and Globular.  
  
Now it's time to take a break from the Psychill/Downtempo genre and try something different. I want to visit the other music style that I love, Psychedelic Trance. And what best way to do that but with the help of one of the legends.  
Bill Halsey, the producer behind Cosmosis (side projects: Abraxas, Mumbo-Jumbo) is a former professional session guitarist from London. During this part of his musical career he played with countless bands playing Rock, Funk, Jazz, Blues, Pop and Hip- Hip, touring Europe and Australasia. Bill honed his writing and recording skills by composing and and recording pop tracks for over ten years before finally becoming involved with creating pure electronic music.  
  
Now much in demand for live appearances to play his particular brand of tongue-incheek, full power Psy-Trance music on dance floors quite literally all over the globe. During the last 20 years of touring Bill has played Cosmosis' music to ecstatic crowds in: Japan, Taiwan, Brazil, Israel, India, Bali, Germany, Australia, New Zealand, Colombia, United States, Egypt, Switzerland, Sweden, Hungary, Slovakia, Denmark, England, Spain, Ireland, Canada, Serbia, Scotland, Belgium, Finland, Holland, Croatia, Greece, South Africa, Russia, France, Mexico and Portugal and plays regularly in many of these countries.  
  
So once again we present you with a 4xCD Box Set. 2 discs will be the Retro compilation, tracks from the Goa Trance era that are hard to find, out of print and unreleased. The other 2 will be the Futurism compilation full of more recent material never printed on CD before.
