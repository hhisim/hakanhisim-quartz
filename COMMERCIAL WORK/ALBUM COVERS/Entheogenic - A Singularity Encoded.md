### **Entheogenic - A Singularity Encoded**

![[752F6D25-B4E2-CF4C-A496-9C8FE81C4715.jpg]]
## Narrative:
The singularity offered by the Originator is received by two Generators, the Aeon Christos and the Aeon Sophia, who balance it between them and dance it into a particular shape. By their combined intensities, they endow the singularity with a set of inherent properties. They encode its pure potential so that its unique properties can unfold in a particular way and eventually give rise to a particular life-form: the Anthropos, the template for the human species.
