## Narrative:
INFINITE SUN was formed in mid 2013 with the intent of combining the heavy riff rock ala Budgie and Mountain with the soundscapes and endless imagination of bands like Hawkwind and Can. Throw in a touch of 70's Blues Rock and 60's Garage Rock. Our main goal is to record and release as much music as possible. Our second album is out now, with number three nearly in the can!
