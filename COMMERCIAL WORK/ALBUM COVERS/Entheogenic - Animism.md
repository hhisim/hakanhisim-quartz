### **Entheogenic - Animism**

![[Entheogenic - Animism.jpg]]
## Narrative:
Entheogenic's 13th Studio Album  
  
“Animism” denotes the action of life not merely as vital force, but a more fully and deeply, animating force. If the animating matrix of life is intelligent, willfull, and responsive to what it produces, it is appropriate to name it and treat it as a living creature having those attributes.
