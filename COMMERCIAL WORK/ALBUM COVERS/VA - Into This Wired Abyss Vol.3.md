### **VA - Into This Wired Abyss Vol.3**

![[565E7DC2-9981-4D46-104B-E6E6E9B16979.jpg]]
## Narrative:
Compiled by [Atman Construct](https://soundcloud.com/atman-construct), **Into This Wired Abyss Vol. 3** is an invitation to seek out untouched sonic cultures, utopian siren songs, and machine dreams of glowing networks in synchronized melodic grooves. Ancient cables, cogs, and pneumatic pistons convulse with fresh virility. Loping wires wind round softly singing circuits and humming hardware, harmonizing, optimizing, and realizing. Long frozen ratchets wake at last to croak their hollow hymns and metallic mating-calls. This digital ecology has come back to life, a cacophony of data is rising, flowing up and out. Come conquistador, join the exploration; this brave new world is yours for the taking. Mastered by [Adam Goodlet @ Re:Creation Studio](http://www.recreationstudio.co.uk/) with artwork by [Hakan Hisim](https://www.facebook.com/hakanhisim).
