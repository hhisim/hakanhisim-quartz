### **Spinal Fusion - Inner Peace**
![[Inner Peace FINAL (web).jpg]]

## Narrative:
Spinal Fusion, brought to you from the mother land of India via BMSS Records, promises to deliver three sonic masterpieces guaranteed to cause a dust storm on the dancefloor. Crystal-clear production, solid basslines and enough psychedelic elements to transport your mind to outer space but with enough groove to keep you feet glued to Earth. The EP consists of two solo tracks and a collaboration with Earbug, all of which have been carefully mastered by Silicon Sound to ensure maximum aural pleasure and presence.
