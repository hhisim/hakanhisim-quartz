## Narrative:
Entheogenic's 7th Studio Album / EP. A tribute to & inspired by cave art 35,000 years old. After a few visits to local caves in the s.w. France & seeing the ancient art in the real & all its stunning humble beauty.. 

The music Anthropomorphic was seeded. After a two year break from the studio, Entheogenic is back with a very unique collection of deep cave emotional psychedelic tracks & songs <3 

(Attention ! This music can be quite challenging at times & requires the full attention of the listener.. particularly on good headphones !)  
  
And a huge very meaningful thanks to [[Hakan Hisim]] for his mind blowing ecstatic eye tantalizing visionary art <3
