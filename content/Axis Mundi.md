## Narrative:
...From between the realms of heaven and earth, and beyond the motion of the rising and descending sun, emanating from within our core essence arises the Axis Mundi.

As the spider spins her web of interconnection from within her existence, allowing the jewel like formation of dew drops to collect on the threads of perfection, she reveals the infinite dimensions of reflection upon Indra's net of creation.

We can feel her connection within the currents of air that surround us, as she whispers our name in the wind if we listen, Gaia shifting and shaping the motions of the unconscious and conscious, not unlike a musician playing instruments of passion.

By focusing on a Divine Yantra, we enter the great silence, the space beyond the chattering of our conditioned mind, igniting the transcension of our heart and spirit, revealing the mandalic space of balance which whispers influence and provides us great guidance.

As they say, only a heart as light as feather can ever reach these higher levels, and a fearless heart is needed to transcend our mind into spirit, for what the world needs now more than ever is the warrior spirits to express this inter-connective message, through art, music, poetry, dance, and love, of this transcendental symphony emanating from the Axis Mundi!...

Written by Alistar Valadez
