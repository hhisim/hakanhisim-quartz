## Narrative:
It had been a while since I had visited this style of art but when the muse tickles your soul the only thing to do is act.  
This is a homage to, and my vision of the love of my life, Elgin, who I and my sons refer to as "Sundaram".  
  
Sundaram is the ultimate beauty, bliss, happiness, contentment, and delight, in which we find a Divine light and presence in all.  
True Beauty is an inner consciousness of bliss (Ananda) where the mind reflects our radiant cosmic inner Being.  
Beauty unveils the dynamism of creativity, aligned with a higher mysticism, idealism, harmony, and wisdom.  
  
When our hearts, minds, and expressions are in sync with the cosmic rhythms, we draw our awareness into the bliss and beauty of Mother Earth and Mother Nature extending into the unknowable Vast beyond.  
A contemplative heart lures the Divine into all our experiences from the ground to the sky.  
Satyam Shivam Sundaram literally means: Truth - Divinity - Beauty  
  
And I am beyond honored and fortunate to share my life with Truth, Divinity, and beauty incarnate in physical form:  
The powerful woman who is my flame and graceful mother of my two children.  
  
The fortunate people who know her realize this image is a pale comparison of her beauty and character, but this is the best I could do for now.  
  
Thank you, my love, my life for you eternal presence, love, and inspiration. ![❤️](https://static.xx.fbcdn.net/images/emoji.php/v9/tf3/1.5/16/2764.png)
