## Narrative:
The irony of humanity is that our individual Unique-ness is very important and very real on one level but on the another level it is fairly illusory. We are all unique and we are all the same. It is a 'Coincidentia Oppositorum' a latin phrase meaning 'coincidence of opposites'. You have to hold these two antithetical things in your mind at once "Unique and Same" in order to correctly perceive the proper level of ambiguity that is resident in reality. It aint simple folks.

**~Terence McKenna**
