## Narrative:
Endless thanks and gratitude to the Mystic Poet **Alistar Valadez**:

..."Deep within a mystical vision there was a face within an image, she said my name is Venus as she showed herself engaged in a battle on a cosmic scale for a position, and she is reaching out to mortal men and women for help in reinvigorating an inner connection to empower our spirit! For we cannot deny that ignorance and unconscious darkness still surround our planet and collective consciousness. Just like in ancient stories, the snake wants to bring us knowledge that separates us from the dark lords who enslave our fellow brothers and sisters. They say as above so below, and it seems that on a cosmic level, the feminine is still over dominated just like man lived previously on the medieval level. It was not long ago women couldn't even vote in some countries, and notice how women are still put down even today in certain areas of the world as if it is normal, which IT still reflects as a mirror on a cosmic level. Still, great spirits can see how treating others as equals is far more beneficial in creating a new world vision of being exalted! If we cannot even do it here, how could we expect it to happen in the outer cosmic ocean!? So we each have our calling, we each have our mission, and every little bit of expressed energy towards the awareness of this process is how we will slowly make it beyond the unconscious repeated patterns. Just like with the perfect song, a passionately expressed message or image can activate new layers of perception! Before witnessing this inspiration our unconscious thoughts can remain locked on a completely different level of vibration, but just one spark can activate a new flame of vision and inspire change to arise from within. We know what is right, and we know our time to rise is now, when we are faced with are darkest moments it is in that space expansion can be found to empower us. Looking up at night we can see Venus is one of the brightest objects like a diamond in the sky, we can see the brilliance of her radiance outshine within the darkness, serving as inspiration that we too can be empowered just as Venus Exalted!"
