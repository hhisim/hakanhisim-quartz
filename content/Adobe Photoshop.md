Each image within this collection shares a connection to Adobe Photoshop, serving as the primary crucible from which the final artwork emerges.

Additionally, Adobe Photoshop is an integral tool utilized alongside Adobe After Effects in nearly every animation project I undertake.

My journey with Adobe Photoshop began with version 2 back in 1998. Having been accustomed to Deluxe Paint during my Amiga days, I found Photoshop to be remarkably intuitive to learn. As I delved deeper into its capabilities, particularly in 2011, I began to unlock its full potential, forever transforming the trajectory of my art.
