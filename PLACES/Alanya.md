  Alanya was a transformative 2.5-year journey for us, a period where we reconnected with family on the Mediterranean coast. It was here that we consciously conceived and experienced a freebirth with our second child.

Although Alanya was not initially ideal for us, it provided comfort during a crucial time in our lives.

It was during our time in Alanya that I truly began to embrace and assert my sovereignty, separating the essential from the extraneous and questioning much of the programming in my life.

Alanya became a fertile ground for creativity, where I produced some of my most widespread and influential pieces. I also delved even deeper into the realms of Universal Transmissions, expanding my artistic and spiritual horizons.
