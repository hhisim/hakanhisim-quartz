## Narrative:
"Aham Brahmasmi" is a profound expression rooted in ancient Indian philosophy, particularly in Advaita Vedanta, where it translates to "I am Brahman" or "I am the universe." This concept suggests that the individual self (atman) is ultimately identical to the transcendent reality (Brahman), indicating the interconnectedness and unity of all existence.
