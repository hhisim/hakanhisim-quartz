The root of all technology is based on and inspired by the esoteric and spiritual realms. Technology is in essence a watered down copy of its original blueprint; Spirituality.  
  
When this realization gains momentum and emerging technologies start to align and converge with true spiritual essence, the resulting symbiosis will usher in nothing short of a golden age.
