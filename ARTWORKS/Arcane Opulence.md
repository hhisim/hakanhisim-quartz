## Narrative:
"Arcane Opulence marks a significant shift in my life journey: on the brink of becoming a first-time father, I made the daring decision to leave behind my stable 9-5 career and embrace the path of a full-time professional artist. This transformative moment gave birth to the iconic artwork, 'Arcane Opulence,' and "[[Singular Sight]]"a crystalline vision where I reimagined the legendary Third Eye of Ra."
