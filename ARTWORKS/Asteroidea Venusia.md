## Narrative:
I had an interesting dream today; I was strolling on a beach under a magenta lit sky, I knew this place to be Venus. On the rocks a small psychedelic starfish

with many blinking eyes beckoned me towards it. As I picked it up, it began sort of melding into my hand and established a telepathic communication channel with me.

I felt a very intensely weird sensation in my mind as it uploaded information into my brain, It was "talking" as it filled my mind with pentagonal geometries and

symbolic imagery. It told me that Starfish, and many other forms of pentagonal based life forms including many plants and fruits of the Earth had originated from, Venus, including my own ancestral origins.

It continued explaining that different geometrical based life forms originated from planets corresponding to their energetic geometry and frequency, most of which I

have not totally forgotten. And concluded that Earth was a cosmic breeding ground for various life throughout the galaxy.

When I awoke, I began trying to capture that strange little cosmic starfish which seemed to exude an air of luxurious dignity coupled with a narcissistic streak. 2 Hours later I came up with this image. It does not do it justice, but it was a worthwhile practice to honor the bizarre memory.
