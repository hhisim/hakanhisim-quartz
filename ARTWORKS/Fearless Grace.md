## Narrative:
A homage to my eternal lady Elgin, love of my immortal soul, and a tribute to the divine goddess archetype; true personification of the warrior spirit that flows

though existence in fearless grace.

The warrior spirit is not gender biased, it is a core ethos that runs through the collective spirit of men and women alike. This tribute is specifically for

all women who face the unknown with unwavering elegance despite insurmountable odds to come out the other side as fierce protectors of life filled with unparalleled wisdom and true empathy.

You are loved.
