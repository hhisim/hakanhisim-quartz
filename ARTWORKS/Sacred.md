## Narrative:
Back in 2013 I created some variants of sacred and cosmic geometrical patterns and symbols. This image "Sacred" was the Zero Point, which evolved in iterations into a set of geometric designs that eventually became the template and fundamental structure that I eventually used consistently throughout my life as a digital artist.
