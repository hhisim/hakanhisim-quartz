Nu-Clear Vision 

The time of being a guest in my body is over. It's Time to walk through the front door of my consciousness, climb my spine, grab my will and reclaim my throne.

Sovereignty, no longer a vague ephemeral concept has taken the front seat on my ride through this cosmos as my journey deeper into self opens up new vista's through healing and transformation.
