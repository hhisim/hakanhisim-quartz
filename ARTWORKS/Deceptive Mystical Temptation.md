No longer hypnotized by the shimmering lights, kaleidoscopic colors, morphing geometries, and promissory glimpses of hyperdimensional havens by self-transforming tykes and mantis overlords. I gazed within and found eternal paradise.

The quest and the search were over and the self as all was realized in divine union.
